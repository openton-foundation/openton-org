What does the Open TON logo symbolize?

The Open TON logo is soaring in infinite space, divided into 2 parts and connected together with a regular tetrahedron with a displaced base. All equal angles and faces of the tetrahedron symbolize universal equality. The rotated base of the tetrahedron symbolizes decentralization and lack of communication with the initiating center. The diverging faces symbolize the infinite possibility of equal expansion. The meaning of the Open TON logo is opposed with the traditional pyramid depicted on US $1, with faces converging to the controlling Eye of Providence. Also, the Open TON logo symbolizes the original crystal, one of the traditional symbols of decentralized digital communities.
