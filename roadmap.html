What is your roadmap? What will be the consensus protocol?

We plan to develop the Open TON based on the original concept of TON Network and TON Blockchain.

What is paramount for the Open TON? What is the first step?

Formation of a large decentralized international community of developers, participants and supporters of Open TON.

How many people do you want to bring to the Open TON community?

At the first stage, we plan to attract more than 1 million people.

When will you launch the Open TON blockchain? When will you launch the Open TON testnet?

We will launch the Open TON testnet within 1 month when our Open TON global community has more than 1 million members.

When is the mainnet launch?

Within 3 months after launching the testnet.

When will the active development of Open TON begin?

After the first stage of the formation of the international community.

What time frame do you set for all further development of the Open TON blockchain?

At the moment, we do not set further deadlines. The decentralized Open TON community will itself begin to determine the timing and roadmap of its development after the community has exceeded 1 million members.

Where is the Open TON code?

In the official OpenTON repository on GitHub.
