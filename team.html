Who are the creators of Open TON? Who are the members of the Open TON team?

Open TON is being launched by a group of independent enthusiasts and developers. We can be called a team, but it is more true to call us the initiators of Open TON.

What are the main tasks of the Open TON initiator team?

Build a large international decentralized community. Launch Open TON Blockchain. Form the conditions for further decentralized development of Open TON.

How to contact the Open TON team?

No way. But you can mention us using the hashtag #openton or nicknames of the official Open TON accounts on your social networks. If there is any important information to us, we will contact you.

Why is the Open TON initiator team anonymous?

It is for decentralization. To avoid the formation of a center or centers of Open TON and minimize the risks of attacks on Open TON developers. The personal anonymity of the Open TON initiator team in this case is the first step towards global decentralization and increased resilience of the entire ecosystem. But in reality, anonymity does not exist. It is a myth.
