In which country is Open TON registered?

At the moment, Open TON as an organization is not registered in any country in the world and in fact is only a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), which exists only on the Internet. This form of decentralized digital community self-organization does not require registration.

Do you plan to register an organization to support Open TON? In what status? In which country?

Right now we do not plan to register an organization. We do not exclude that this will need to be done in the future. If required, Open TON will be registered as a charitable organization. This can be done in digital and crypto friendly countries. If necessary, a charitable organization may be registered in the United States.

What do you think about the laws in different countries in regulating the digital economy, blockchain projects and cryptocurrencies?

If some activity requires regulation, so be it. Open TON does not set itself goals and objectives to actively resist the regulation of some countries. Open TON will be developed in accordance with international regulatory rules.

If you are not against government regulation, what is the point of launching Open TON?

Unfortunately, in most countries of the world, government mechanisms suppress the digital community. It should not be so. Open TON will contribute to the development of an open, decentralized international community as the fundamental foundation of the international digital economy and progress.

What do you think about the US regulation? Do you plan to fight against the SEC?

We believe that the United States is putting undue pressure on digital progress. We do not agree with the US policy of suppressing the global economy. But we do not plan to fight against the USA and SEC. We will seek the formation of new international standards by all legal means. The state should be subordinate to society, and not society to the state.

Do I need to go through the KYC procedure now? Will I need to undergo such procedures in the future?

Open TON is now distributing coins for free. To get them, you do not need to go through the KYC procedure, because Open TON coins have no value or price. But we do not exclude that further passage of such procedures may be required.

Can I anonymously get free Open TON coins? It is legal?

Yes, you can anonymously get free Open TON coins. For most countries of the world this is legal. Until they have no price. Until you try to exchange them for money.

I am a resident of the USA. Can I become a member of the Open TON community? Can I get free Open TON coins? Are there any restrictions on the participation of US residents in the Open TON community?

US friendly. Open TON does not restrict US residents to receive free Open TON coins and does not prohibit them from participating in the development of the project.

There are extremely severe restrictions in my country, can I get free Open TON coins and participate in the development of the Open TON community?

In most cases, you can. For the vast majority of countries in the world, participating in the development of an international decentralized digital community is not a violation of the law. If in doubt, please consult a lawyer. Responsibility for this is on your side.

Why now Open TON coin has no value and price?

Because the Open TON project has not yet been launched. Any value can be formed only through the active participation of the community in the development of the project in the future. Any value of coins right now, any planning of value, a promise of value carries regulatory risks. This does not mean that coins cannot have value later. But that means coins are priceless right now.

Do I have to pay taxes on received Open TON coins?

Now owning Open TON coins is not subject to taxation in most countries of the world. You do not need to declare and pay taxes on digital assets that do not have value, prices and cannot be valued in any way. If in the future Open TON coin has a price, you may need to pay taxes, according to the regulations of the country of your tax residency. In this case, you will need to consult your tax consultant or lawyer. If you think that in your country you should declare Open TON coins now, consult a lawyer.

Do you collect my personal data?

No, we do not collect your personal data. We are not an operator of the processing and storage of personal data. We only have the nickname of your Telegram account which you used to create your Open TON account. To provide additional security for your Open TON account, you can also use any email without any personal data. But this is not mandatory.

Can I leave the Open TON project and delete my account?

As long as your Open TON coins are stored by @OpenTONbot, you can leave the Open TON project at any time by simply deleting your account in a special section of the bot settings. Please note that free Open TON coins will also be deleted from your account, you will lose access to them and the balance cannot be restored.
