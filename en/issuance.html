Will the Open TON have its own coin?

Yes.

What will it be called? What ticker will it have?

This is not known right now. The community itself will vote for the name and ticker of the Open TON coin in the future. At the moment, we call it the Open TON coin.

Is Open TON coin a cryptocurrency? What is its value?

Open TON coin is a digital coin for the new digital decentralized Open TON world. At the moment, they have no value and price. Now Open TON coins are priceless.

How will the Open TON coins be used in the future?

The Open TON coins will be used to ensure the functioning of the Open TON blockchain.

What will be the nominal price of the Open TON coin?

Now the Open TON coin has no value and no nominal price.

Will the Open TON coin be traded on exchanges?

If the Open TON coin has value and price in the future, they can be traded on exchanges.

Where can I buy or sell the Open TON coins now?

Nowhere. Now Open TON coins are not bought or sold anywhere.

Will the Open TON coins be distributed free of charge?

Yes. Right now, Open TON coins are distributed free of charge to members of the Open TON community.

How can I get the Open TON coins for free now?

You need to create your Open TON account on the Telegram via @OpenTONbot and get your Open TON coins for free.

I do not have a Telegram account, where can I get the Open TON coins for free?

Now, we only give away free Open TON coins through Telegram. If you want to get the coins now, you need to create a Telegram account.

How many Open TON coins can I get for free?

More than 100 coins. 50 Open TON coins for registration + 25 Open TON coins for completing tasks + 10 Open TON coins for each new member you bring to the community using your referral link.

What is the Open TON coin limit I can get for free?

There is currently no limit. But the limit on free Open TON coins can be set in the future to avoid centralization. If you DO NOT agree to the condition that the amount of free Open TON coins may be limited, you SHOULD NOT join the Open TON community.

What will be the issuance of Open TON coins in total?

Currently, the issuance size of Open TON coins has not yet been determined. It will be determined in the future through an open community voting.

What percentage of the Open TON coin issued (will be issued) is for free distribution?

It has not yet been determined.

Why now do you not have answers to many important questions?

Because the community is not yet formed. There is still no decentralization. The decentralized community must itself provide answers to the most important questions through an open voting in the future.

Where will my Open TON coins be stored, which I will receive for free?

They will be stored in your personal @OpenTONbot account on Telegram. Then you can withdraw them to your wallet in the Open TON blockchain.

When can I withdraw my coins from @OpenTONbot to my Open TON wallet?

After launching the Open TON blockchain.

What can I do with the Open TON coins I received now?

Prior to launching mainnet, you can only store and accumulate coins in @OpenTONbot on Telegram. You need to wait for the launch of the Open TON blockchain.

Someone stole my Open TON coins from @OpenTONbot, what should I do?

It is impossible. Coins cannot be withdrawn or transferred from @OpenTONbot. You can lose access to them only if you lose access to your Telegram account. You need to safeguard your Telegram account to protect your Open TON coins.

Why now can everyone get Open TON coins for free?

Because we are forming a truly global decentralized digital community.
