Who are the partners of Open TON?

We will announce any partnerships only on the OpenTON.org website. Statements by any organizations that they are partners of Open TON will not be true if OpenTON.org does not support such statements.

How can my organization become an Open TON partner?

You need to start actively promoting the development of Open TON.

Do you know about other decentralized communities? Are you in them?

Yes, we know. Yes, we are in some of them. We are for decentralization. None of the decentralized communities can be the main. No decentralized community can be a center of decentralization, consider itself as such, declare it, lay claim to it, or somehow restrict other decentralized communities, or interfere with their development. Absolute global decentralization must prevail. A decentralized community must control the world.

Can I be a member of the Open TON community and also be a member of other centralized and decentralized communities?

Sure you can.
